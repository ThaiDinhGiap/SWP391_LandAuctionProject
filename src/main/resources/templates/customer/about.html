Introduction

VietLand website would like to send our best regards to our Customers.

In the strong development trend of the "Auction" field, VietLand has been established with the support of a Council of Advisors including experienced price and lawyer experiences. Operated by the Knight Value Expert of the Ministry of Justice, we are proud to be one of 18 professional auction units in Hanoi, recognized by the Hanoi Department of Justice in report No. 35/BC-STP, dated August 15, 2011. We are also the only unit to be awarded a commendation by the Chairman of the Hanoi People's Committee for "achievements in judicial support activities" in 2014" on March 19, 2015.

VietLand website is a member of Lac Viet Group, owning a system of Notary Offices and Law Firms with high expertise. This effectively supports our property auction activities. VietLand is committed to providing our customers with the best quality and most effective services.

We know that you are in need of consulting and organizing property auctions. Therefore, we would like to send a proposal introducing the capacity of VietLand Website, with the hope of being selected as the organization to auction the product assets for you.

With the motto "Bringing outstanding economic efficiency", we hope to satisfy you and wish to accompany you throughout the process of operation and development.

“We want to go ahead, to be at the forefront so that when the world looks at it, they will also see that in Vietnam there are reputable auction houses, they will also send assets here for us to sell, or invite us to cooperate abroad. The road is still very long, we are just like a baby learning to walk.

I just hope VietLand will be the leading ship, the more ships in the future, the more fun, reaching the destination together, the ship in front will block the waves for the ship behind, safely overcoming the big waves”
